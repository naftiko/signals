Outcomes

Our goal with this workshop is to have you walk way with more questions and confidence in understanding how being able to see and quantify the services you depend upon can help you manage cost, velocity, and risk.

Costs

  • Maximize Your Existing API Investment - Realize the importance of embracing your legacy investements.
  • Tracing Business Boundaries - Establish meaningful boundaries around the servies that you are using.
  • Integrate Into FinOps - Establish a FinOps program, or enrich existing with service domains.

Velocity

  • Tap iInto That Desire to Move Faster - There is a lot of fuel available, you just need to properly allocate.
  • Properly Define Velocity - Take a moment to make sure you have a shared understanding of what velocity means.
  • Right-Size Velocity - Ensure that you are establishing the right velocity for different domains and scenarios.

Risk

  • Managing Risk is Priority - Understand that finding solutions requires a committment to managing risk.
  • Reduce Risk in Gaps - Knowing that risk accumulates in the places you can't see across multiple domains.
  • Know Where Risk Is - If you have a handle on what your risk is, you can properly assess what priorities are.

Innovation

  • Right-Sized Context - Providing the context needed to deliver the AI agents, copilots, and automated required.
  • Balanced Approach - Moving confidently, cautiously, and in balanced ways across domains and teams who are aligned.
  • Governed Integration - Confidently innovating because you know that risk is properly managed across domains.

Our intent is to help elevate you above the forest in which you operate to give you some practice, to give you a new view of the landscape, leaving you thinking more about what is your priority, and what is next.
